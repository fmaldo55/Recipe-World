Perfectly Preserved Pie

Ingredients

Pie Base

  • 16 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp vanilla bean
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • hot water

Strawberry Sauce

  • 2 lbs strawberries, hulled
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 3 tbsp water

Buttercream Caramel

  • 1/2 cup butter, room temperature
  • 1/4 cup caramel sauce
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1-2 cups confectioners' sugar

Instructions

Cheesecake Filling

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  4. Add sugar, sour cream, salt, cornstarch, vanilla bean, and heavy cream. Mix until well combined.
  5. Place the pie base in a baking pan and pour the cheesecake filling over it.
  6. Place the baking pan in a larger pan and fill the larger pan with hot water halfway up the sides of the baking pan.
  7. Bake for 1 hour or until the center is set.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool to room temperature.
  9. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.

Strawberry Sauce

  1. In a saucepan, combine strawberries, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt.
  2. In a separate small bowl, mix cornstarch and water until smooth.
  3. Add the cornstarch mixture to the saucepan and stir well.
  4. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens and the strawberries break down, about 10-15 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  6. Once cooled, refrigerate until ready to serve.

Buttercream Caramel

  1. In a mixing bowl, beat the butter until creamy.
  2. Add the caramel sauce and salt. Mix well.
  3. Add confectioners' sugar gradually until the desired consistency is reached, mixing well after each addition.
  4. Spread the buttercream caramel over the chilled cheesecake.
